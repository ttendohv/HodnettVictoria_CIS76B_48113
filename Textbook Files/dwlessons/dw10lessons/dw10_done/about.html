The Sounds of Nature Recording Series

The Sounds of Nature Recording Series is a soothing collection of music that could only be possible with the help of the natural world. Lush soundscapes are set against meticulous field recordings to give tlisteners a relaxing and meditative experiende in the comfort of their own homes.

Imagine a forest with leaves stirring gently in an autumn breeze or a deserted beach on a summer evening. The Sounds of Nature Recording Series lets you experience these picturesque moments any time of the day.
